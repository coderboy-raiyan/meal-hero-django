About us

Owner Raiyan

Welcome to Meal Hero, a culinary haven where tradition meets innovation. Nestled in the heart of Dhaka, Bangladesh, our restaurant is a testament to Raiyan's passion for food and his dedication to serving authentic Bangladeshi cuisine with a modern twist. Raiyan's culinary journey began in his grandmother's kitchen, where he was captivated by the symphony of flavors and aromas that emerged from her cooking. Inspired by her legacy, Raiyan embarked on a mission to preserve the essence of Bangladeshi cuisine while infusing it with his own creativity and flair.
